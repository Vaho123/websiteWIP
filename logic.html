Puzzle Time


Four friends are on a trip to a theme park. Use the clues below (scroll down) to determine the name, the favourite rollercoaster ride, hat colour, snack to be eaten on the ride and height of each daredevil.

Each daredevil is in a specific position, wearing a specific coloured hat, eating a specific snack and has a specific favourite rollercoaster ride - but which is which?

Make a note of the answers to these questions before submitting.

    Who is wearing the blue hat?
    Whose favourite rollercoaster is Lightning?
    Who will eat cake on the ride?
